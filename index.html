⭐ Comprehensive Features

Advanced GPA Tools

Discover the most advanced GPA & CGPA calculation, planning, and reporting features—fully customizable and designed for every university system.

Semester GPA Calculator

Input course marks (manual or via templates) to compute GPA, percentage, average marks, total credits, and obtain a polished PDF report.

Cumulative GPA Calculator

Track academic progress across semesters, with options to include/exclude credit hours. Supports 4.0, 5.0, and 10.0 scales, plus detailed CGPA breakdown and PDF export.

Strategic GPA Planner

Set a target CGPA and calculate the required semester GPAs for the remaining terms. Includes achievability analysis to assess your goals.
